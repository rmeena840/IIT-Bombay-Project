Multiple lenses experiment concepts

About the experiment

The experiment shows a how can we calculate Equivalent power of Multiple lenses.

Power

The power of a lens is defined as the reciprocal of its focal length in meters, or D = 1/f, where D is the power in diopters and f is the focal length in meters.

The Formula:

Power if given as, P= 1/f

Equivalent Power of lens is give as, P=P(1)+P(2).....

Points to remember:

* Concave lens have negative focal length.

* Convex lens have positive focal length.
